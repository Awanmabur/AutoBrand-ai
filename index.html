Common questions

How do I create a brand kit?

Add your audience, products, tone, offers, sample captions, brand rules and visual direction in the brand workspace.

Can agencies manage clients?

Yes. Agency workspaces are designed for multiple brand profiles, approvals and repeatable campaign systems.

Can I export content?

Yes. Campaigns can be copied, exported or prepared for scheduling tools and social media workflows.

What should I add to a campaign brief?

Include objective, product, audience, offer, deadline, tone, channel, key proof points and preferred content formats.

How does brand memory help?

It keeps repeated outputs aligned with your saved tone, audience, offers, content pillars and client-specific rules.

Can my team review content before publishing?

Yes. Use statuses such as draft, in review, approved and scheduled to organize the content pipeline.

Privacy policy.

How Classic AI Autobrand handles account details, brand workspaces, campaign content and support information.

Ask a question
Last updated: May 21, 2026Applies to website and app users

Overview

This privacy policy explains how Classic AI Autobrand may collect, use and protect information when users create accounts, build brand workspaces, generate campaign content or contact support.

The goal of this policy is to make data handling clear for creators, marketing teams, agencies and businesses using the platform to manage brand content.

Information we collect

Depending on how the platform is used, we may collect the following types of information:

  • Account details such as name, email address and login information.
  • Brand workspace data such as brand voice, audience notes, product descriptions, campaign briefs and content preferences.
  • Generated content such as captions, scripts, calendars, ad angles and campaign notes.
  • Support information such as messages, requests, feedback and troubleshooting details.
  • Basic usage data that helps improve performance, reliability and product experience.

How we use information

Information is used to provide the service, personalize AI outputs, maintain account access, improve product quality, respond to support requests and keep the platform secure.

  • To create and manage user accounts and brand workspaces.
  • To generate more relevant content based on saved brand context.
  • To provide customer support, onboarding and troubleshooting.
  • To understand product usage and improve platform reliability.

Brand and campaign content

Brand kits, campaign briefs and generated outputs are treated as user workspace content. Users should avoid uploading sensitive information unless it is necessary for their workflow.

Sharing and disclosure

We do not sell user content. Information may be shared only when needed to operate the service, comply with legal obligations, prevent abuse or support integrations requested by the user.

Security

Reasonable technical and organizational safeguards should be used to protect accounts and workspace data. Users are responsible for keeping passwords secure and controlling access to their accounts.

User choices

Users may request updates, exports or deletion of account information where applicable. Some data may be retained when required for legal, security or operational reasons.

Users can also choose what brand information they add to the platform and should remove outdated product, audience or campaign details when they are no longer needed.

Contact

For privacy questions, contact the Classic AI Autobrand team through the Contact page.
